My Obsession for Cats

Studying cats is a passion of mine. It started from when I was younger and my parents had two cats named Pepper and Freddy. Pepper was what most people would call a cow cat black and white, Freddy was all light grey. Freddy was an old cat who did not enjoy attention who hated children but Pepper was an excellent cat who enjoyed any kind of attention. As a kid I was fascinated by how Pepper was so chill and friendly. Eventually both cats passed away, Freddy to old age and Pepper had a heart attack. The next couple of cats were Josie and Poco.
